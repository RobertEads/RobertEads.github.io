About Me

My love of technology started the moment I got my first laptop in 6th grade and has not stopped growing. I built my first computer during my sophmore year of high school and have been tinkering with any technology I can get my hands on ever since.

My first experience with coding was the summer after 7th grade at a University of Texas at Dallas coding camp. That exploration got me hooked on coding and after that I took just about every computer science class my school offered when I got to high school. I am now a student at Texas A&M University studying for my undergraduate degree in computer science.
