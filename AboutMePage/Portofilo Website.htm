About Me

My name is Robert Eads and I am 20 years old. I was born and raised in the Dallas, Texas area and I whole hearted embrace Texas culture. My hobbies include running, playing basketball, and listening to music.

My love of technology started the moment I got my first laptop in 6th grade and has not stopped growing. I built my first computer during sophmore year of high school and have been tinkering with any technology I can get my hands on ever since. My first experience with programming was the summer after 7th grade at a University of Texas at Dallas coding camp. That exploration got me hooked on programming and afterwards I took just about every computer science class my high school had to offer. Now, I am a student at Texas A&M University studying for my B.S. in computer science.
